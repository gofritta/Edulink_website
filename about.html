About Us

High school is a crucial phase in students' lives, where finding the right plan to succeed in the baccalaureate, including choosing courses in appropriate educational settings. The goal of our website, EduLink aka Educational Link , is to provide the support to high school students during their academic journey, guiding them towards suitable educational opportunities. EduLink, Allows students to search for a private school . Additionally, our platform allow student to leave their feedbacks and what they think about a particular school allowing other students to know more about the school. Furthermore, students can check schedules , other closures and write a report if there is a problem with the site. Our website is not only for students it also aims to help private schools to represent their services among its services add teacher , add planning and allows student to register online . When you follow the right path by choosing the private school and the right teacher, this means success in the baccalaureate.
